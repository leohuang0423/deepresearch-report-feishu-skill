# Non-Consensus Insight Rules

Use this module when the report needs real `insight` and `foresight`, not consensus paraphrase.

## Required Pattern

For each major insight, write:

- `consensus_view`
- `thesis`
- `first_principles_basis`
- `evidence_refs`
- `falsification_path`

## Standard

- At least two insights should feel non-obvious to an expert reader.
- Novelty alone is not enough; the claim must still be bounded and evidence-backed.
- If you cannot separate your thesis from consensus clearly, the insight is too weak.

## Anti-Patterns

- "X is getting more important" with no discriminating judgment
- calling a summary an insight
- future predictions with no mechanism chain or kill condition
