# Local-Only Synthesis Rules

Use `local_only_synthesis` when the task is high-value and report-shaped, but the evidence boundary is intentionally narrow and external research would be fake, low-yield, or out of scope.

## Use This Route When

- the packet is a playbook, operating memo, review standard, or method synthesis
- the allowed inputs are mostly local task artifacts or user-provided notes
- the audience still needs a sharp report, not a casual note
- traceability matters, but full market-proof or creator-first sourcing does not apply

## Core Principle

Do not pretend a bounded synthesis task is a multi-source research report.

Preserve the contract discipline:

- explicit audience
- explicit decision goal
- explicit ideal-state standard
- explicit boundary notes
- explicit falsification path

But compress the heavy-report machinery into the final packet instead of emitting every intermediate artifact as separate files.

## Required Compression

If the task only allows a small artifact set, preserve these states inside `appendix.md` or `run_log.json`:

- thesis card
- evidence map
- outline freeze
- figure roles
- review and acceptance notes

## Writing Standard

- keep the main report reader-first and answer-first
- keep the appendix as the audit layer
- state clearly whether the packet is analytic synthesis, empirical review, or both
- ban pseudo-empirical language when no external evidence exists

## What Not To Do

- do not invent case studies or source objects
- do not require publish assets unless the task explicitly asks for them
- do not let contract compliance make the main body feel like a checklist dump
- do not hide local-only limits; make them legible
